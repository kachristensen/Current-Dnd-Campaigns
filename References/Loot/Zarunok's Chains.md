Zaronok/Zarunok’s Chains

These black iron chains coil and slither with a will of their own. They bind to their master’s very soul, linking them to the rigid dominion of infernal law.

Outer Essence.  This item radiates essence from an outer plane. While attuned, this essence extends to its wearer.

Blessings of Tyranny.  While attuned to this artifact, you gain the following features:

Infernal Fortitude.  While attuned to this artifact, you have Resistance to Fire damage. 

Chain Strike.  When you take the attack action on your turn, you may make an additional attack using this artifact.  It functions as a simple melee weapon with a range of 30 feet and deals 1d4 bludgeoning damage.  You are considered proficient with this weapon.

The Devil You Know.  While attuned to this artifact, you grow horns and gain the ability to speak infernal.  You also have Disadvantage on Stealth Checks, as you are compelled to present yourself as an arbiter of law and authority.

Chains of Domination (Recharge 1d4 Rounds).  As a bonus action, you can send the chains slithering toward a creature within 30 feet. The target must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or have the Restrained condition.  The chains prevent a creature bound by them from using any method of extradimensional movement, including teleportation or travel to a different plane of existence. They don't prevent the creature from passing through an interdimensional portal.  A creature bound by the chains can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns, freeing itself on a success.  You can release the target by letting go of your end of the chains (causing them to coil up in the target's space) or by using a Bonus Action to repeat the command (causing the chains to coil up in your hand).  Only one creature can be Restrained in this manner at a time.



First Upgrade / Awakened:

Blessings of Tyranny: Upon awakening, this artifact gains the following features:

Infernal Insight: While attuned to this artifact, you have Advantage on Intimidation and Insight ability checks.

Indomitable Will.  You may cast the Command spell without expending a spell slot, up to three times each day.  The artifact regains these uses daily at dawn.

Might Makes Right.  While attuned to this artifact, your alignment becomes Lawful Evil.  You also have Disadvantage on Sleight of Hand and Deception ability checks, as your authority is absolute and you see no reason to debase yourself with lies and tricks.

Chain Strike.  The artifact’s damage increases to 2d4.

Chains of Domination.  The saving throw DC for this artifact increases to 18




Second Upgrade / Exalted:

Blessings of Tyranny: Upon exaltation, this artifact gains the following features:

Devilish Mastery.  While attuned to this artifact, you may ignore the effects of one detrimental property.

The Road to Hell.  While attuned to this artifact, your Creature Type is Fiend.  Additionally, you have Vulnerability to damage from silvered weapons.

Chain Strike.  The artifact’s damage increases to 4d4.

Chains of Domination.  The saving throw DC for this artifact increases to 23

Infernal Judgment. 

You can call upon the god of devils to pass judgment on a creature you can see. The target makes a DC 23 Charisma saving throw. On a failure, they are dragged into a pocket dimension of terror and dread by spectral chains, taking 12d12 of the player’s choice of fire, lightning, poison, ice, thunder, or acid damage.  In addition, the creature has Disadvantage on attack rolls, saving throws, and ability checks until the end of their next turn.  On a successful save, a creature takes half damage and has disadvantage on attack rolls, and Strength and Dexterity saving throws until the end of their next turn.

Once you use this feature, you can’t use it again until you finish a Long Rest.
