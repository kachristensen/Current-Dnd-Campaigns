###### Day 33
## Summary
The party continued east and fought monsters in a cave.

## IC Synopsis - Lewis
>We slipped out of the lair through the rear entrance and emerged into the moat, with Valindra leading us like a ghost. Thanks to her skill, we remained unseen as we made our way toward the city. The moat gradually blended into the surrounding terrain, leading east toward the temple protected by the great shield. Along the way we discovered what appeared to be an old sewer system, and I could only hope it was still intact enough to provide safe passage. Before we entered, Valindra silently dispatched a lone zombie without alerting the others, allowing us to continue deeper without incident.
  Inside the sewers, Valindra spotted a creature lurking ahead and called out a warning. I rushed forward, crushing it with my mace while Rem joined me, cutting into the beast with sword and chain. Moments later, Valindra discovered another tunnel crawling with more enemies, and the fight erupted. Ria unleashed devastating bursts of magic, while Persephone summoned glowing fairies that illuminated the larger Sorrowsworn hiding in the darkness. Valindra and Wuffi both suffered painful wounds as the monsters pressed their attack, so I stepped forward, drawing the creature's attention and offering words of encouragement that helped steady Valindra. Together we slowly turned the tide—Rem carved through the monsters with relentless strikes, Valindra and Wuffi finished one foe together, Ria battered another with spell after spell, and after a string of crushing critical blows from all of us, the final Sorrowsworn was utterly destroyed.
  With the tunnels finally quiet, I offered a Prayer of Healing, giving everyone a chance to recover before we continued. Not long after, we encountered a floating Whistler whose eerie song intensified the moment it spotted us. Valindra charged first, only to recoil from its haunting cry as it summoned two Striders from the darkness. The strange creatures extinguished the light radiating from my mace, forcing us to fight nearly blind. Rem landed a devastating critical strike, Persephone supported us with crossbow fire, and together we brought the Striders down one by one. Just as victory seemed within reach, the Whistler teleported away and fled at incredible speed. Rem immediately gave chase, followed by Ria and Valindra, while I realized I had been left behind and sprinted after the rest of the party.
  The pursuit led us straight into another brutal battle. Before we could catch the fleeing Whistler, other horrors descended upon it, tearing it apart as a Shadow Mauler and a Shadow Horror emerged from the darkness. Rem stood his ground against the Mauler while the rest of us intercepted the Horror. I forced my way through the melee but failed to land my first swing, though Ria obliterated the Horror moments later with overwhelming magic, celebrating her triumph. Rem continued dismantling the Mauler while Persephone struck from range, and I delivered the final crushing blow. Valindra swiftly dispatched the last remaining threat, leaving the tunnels silent once more. Though victorious, we pressed onward into the darkness, knowing more enemies lay ahead. I could only hope we would all make it out alive.
