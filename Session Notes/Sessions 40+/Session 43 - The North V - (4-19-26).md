## IC Synopsis - Valindra
>Not a moment had passed from when the facade of these beautiful women fell away to disgusting creatures as they fell, did our enemy send their next wave. It had appeared that our fight with the ghost and drowned women was being watched from the shadows. Lewis and I stood there with our strength sapped just to be run down by these tiny fey creatures. They were quick and nimble, sometimes it felt as if I was just swinging at the air about them.
  As they and the snow elves closed in, I placed myself in the choke point. Hopefully giving Lewis time to get to Rem who was still feeling the affects of the ghostly gaze. To our luck, Rem financially shook off the effect the ghost had landed on him. With him newly recovered we were able to fight back this next wave with ease. 
  Beaten and weakend we fell back to the cave entrance. Finding that it had frozen over solid. Rhanka had seemed to have it in her mind that we were escaping. Perhaps her father was right in a sense that she is too quick to give up. Rather we collected ourselves in one of the chambers off shooting the entrance. After ten minutes of prayer, Lewis was able to refresh the group of us. It had seemed though the strength stolen from us was not so easily recovered.
  A strange feeling that was. Being wrapped up and unable to move as these women kissed me. In my vision, a kiss from Vera made my knees weaken. But the weakness from those deceptive women was a different weakness. One that even lingers. Perhaps Persephone could tell me why kissing leaves one weak. 
  After finding we are more healthy than not, we proceeded down the path. Coming across a small fry creature made of stone. Korrid it called itself, and rather than outright attacking us, it took a moment to speak. Trying to warn us from continuing and to turn back. It seemed unwilling to fight us. Persephone noticed this as well and posed that very question to the talkative fey.
  Korrid told us that he was under some form of magic from the matron compelling then to fight for her. Telling us he does not wish to fight us. But will do so if we step further. Having understood their unwillingness, it was collectively understood not to kill him. It appears many of those we are fighting are enslaved. 
  As we stepped forward, Korrid attacked, along with two more of his ilk. They were quick strong. And their hide thick. My performance is this battle was terrible. I watched over and over my glaive bouncing off the stone hide as if I were cutting into a mountain itself. The others seemed to be doing better, their blows landing with more purchase. Rhanka moving forward around the current combatants found more enemies coming upon us. These women had blue skin and appeared immune to the ice run on Rhanka's axe. Just as they found their magics finding no answer for Rhanka as well. The blue women turned to us.
  As they came in, my frustrations were growing. Recalling some knowledge of these stone creatures, I remembered that their connection to the earth is what strengthens them. Wuffi, still weak himself attempted to lift one of the ground, but failing to the strength of these creatures. Seeing this the group of creatures like Korrid surrounded Wuffi, felling him where he stood. 
  As Wuffi fell, the air around me began to feel colder. Pain from the cold started to stab at my arms and legs. The blue women seemed to emit a dangerous coldness around them. Without out Wuffi to mute to the effects of Etifa's heart, the cold aura became an immediate threat. I retreated back to Ria and Persephone only to be struck down with a ranged attack. 
  As Persephone attempted to conceal herself administering her flask to me, Ria unleashed a power blast of fire. Those blue women seemed susceptible to the flames, quickly this one spell turned the tide of the battle. Rem and Rhanka quickly bringing the rest down. Making sure the fey creatures only to lose their wits rather than their lives. 
  With the field cleared yet again Lewis saw to healing my wounds. Quickly patching me up, and Rem seeing to his own wounds. Moving to the next room we attempted to sneak past to the matron's chamber. But, perhaps due to how tired we were becoming we were made. More snow elves prepared to fight us. Just as we drew our weapons to defend ourselves, from the shadows of the room a giant creature stepped forward. A large giant type fey creature carrying a pot in front of it....wait.... That isn't a pot, that cylinder of liquid is a part of it!
