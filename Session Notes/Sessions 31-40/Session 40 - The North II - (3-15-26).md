## Summary
The party killed the snow elf ambush with the hag.

## IC Synopsis - Frey
> The snowfall in the area picked up following the disappearance of the hag’s projection, and we were soon accosted by more skeletal ice giants. These were soon joined by a snow elf ambush, and later the hag and a decidedly miserable eladrin. 
   Rhenka seemed to have recovered her fighting spirit well enough in the moment, dealing decisive blows to the undead during this engagement, as well as some of the elves. Lewis, newly empowered, distributed Blessings and healing alike. It is early yet, but I am wary certain decisions of his will inevitably come back to bite us. 
   After their initial assault, the elves withdrew, meaning to draw us in toward the hag and eladrin— a trap we were warned of thanks to translation by Valindra. Between the Darkness the snow elves cast, and manipulations of the weather by the hag and eladrin, control of the battlefield remained in their hands until we started felling them. My new summon proved useful enough in assisting that endeavor to be worth employing again in the future, I believe.
   Rembrandt and Persephone in particular took the brunt of harm from our enemies, and were briefly separated from the rest of us by an Ice Wall cast from the hag. She and the eladrin seemingly had few qualms about killing or hindering their own. Perhaps this is something we can use, if we do manage to speak to any of these snow elves. 
   Lewis dealt the killing blow to the hag, following an attempt to flee with the eladrin which I was luckily able to Command to a halt. Rem, in turn, finished off the eladrin. 
   In the aftermath of the battle, Persephone collected ears from the fallen as proof of each kill, while Lewis gathered their equipment under my instruction. Admittedly, it is possible better proof could have been collected, if for nothing else than Valindra’s comfort, but it is rather too late for that now. 
   Of note, Lewis discovered another totem in the chest cavity of the hag, similar to the one discovered previously. Unusual, and interesting. I must wonder at what exactly this Matron is, that can puppet even a powerful hag.
