###### Day 31 - 32
## Summary
The party made a plan of attack to enter Valdrheim and fought undead.

## IC Synopsis - Valindra
> With our first scuffle of Valdrheim behind us, Persephone made the suggestion for us to retreat to calmer lands to inquire with Hypithia. I think her course of thinking was the right of it. We already ran into such fearsome creatures and we were not at the city walls yet, and we were going in blind. 
   Finding a quite clearing we settled down for the night, taking the peace to ask Hypithia more about Valdrheim itself. Hypithia was able to draw us out a map of the city and providing us with three points of interest. To the East we would find one of the artifacts that are to be hidden in the city. To the north lay the home of the one presumably at cause of the terrors in this land. A man cursed by these horrid magics. Hypithia did not give us more on the motivations of this person, but they also had an artifact in their possession. Lastly, to the west Hypithia noted that information can be found for Persephone and her goals finding her son. A surprise to us all but not more than Persephone herself. 
   As we noted down the map provided to use we discussed our course of action. All agreeing to head to the north. Hoping that quelling the lost soul of this man would tame the undead of this land. As we stepped foot on those lands the creatures to me felt organized. Like an army. Sentries posted about, as well as creatures in the air patrolling the skies. As well still, for a land of death, the ground itself felt... alive. As if it was moving beneath our feet. It all felt connected, and after speaking with Hypithia it made the most sense to try and undo this connection first. 
   Our night in Persephone's dome was uneventful. I pondered my thoughts as we sat there through the night. Though was did catch my eye was in the sky above. Or rather what was missing. Too often would the stars vanish from my sight. Something large was passing over head, hiding the night sky from my vision. I recall us being told of a giant dragon, brought back from the grave. With Wuffi's ill fated encounter with the skeletal Roc and this abomination of draconic kind, we should stay firmly on the ground. Escaping through the sky like we did over the river, should only be a choice of last resort. 
   We left early the next morning, passing through the previous day's battlefield. Finding the corpses remaining from the last encounter. Coming upon the city's walls, we found a rampart that had been destroyed, giving us a clear path into the city. As we approached this opening we were quickly met with fallen soldiers rising from their graves. As well as many other undead creatures both on the walls and at our level. 
   Persephone started the battle of with a powerful slow spell. Encapsulating the majority of the horde that was directly in front of us. With Rem, Ria and Lewis ready to start their assaults on these weakened horrors, I turned to the western part of the wall. Aiming for the fallen soldier on the ground, and the enemies on the rampart raining down arrows on us. With Ria's help the two on the ground fell quickly, as the others started taking out many of the creatures caught in the slow spell. I watched as Lewis brought his mace down one of the creatures, exploding it into brilliant light. 
   My attention turned to the top of the rampart. Climbing the beaten up ladder to face the wraith creature raining arrows on my friends. Facing it up high, I used all my strength shove this enemy off the rampart and to the ground. Wuffi turned his attention to a powerful undead that was skilled in magic. Which we quickly learned it could fly, and Wuffi began pursuit across the battlefield after it. The wraith unhappy with my sending it to terra firma, climbed the ladder to enact it's revenge on me. Quickly, I turned to throwing the ladder to the ground crushing the enemy beneath it. 
   As I watched from above, Lewis held tight to his holy symbol around his neck, and called out for his divine influence. Frightening many of the remaining undead. As Rem Ria and Persephone finished off them one by one, Lewis, like a sheep dog, corralled the undead. Chasing them from one end of the field to the other, until myself and the others dispatched the rest. 
   As I was quite beaten from this encounter, we took a moment to rest and recover before entering the city proper. Once my strength was back, we stepped into the city square. I attempted to mask our presence with my magics. This was fruitless, as we came close to a dead tree in the neighborhood center, another of those ghostly dryad spirits emerged. Its horrible scream sounded out. The ground beneath our feet seemed to come alive like before, and sounds of wailing and monsters began to fill our ears. It was if the entire city was coming to us...
